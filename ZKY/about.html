Arrival by taxi

The address is on the right. Print it, take a photo of it or copy it to your mobile device for showing it to the taxi driver. The taxi will leave you at the campus gate at 100 Zhangwu Road, from where you’ll need to walk a couple of minutes following the signs pointing to Sino-Finnish Centre. Once you arrive at our front door, take the elevator to the 4th floor. From Pudong airport the ride should cost less than 200rmb, from downtown about 40rmb.

Arrival by metro

Take Metro Line 10 to Tongji University Station, and come out from exit 2. Walk ahead along Zhangwu road and turn left twice from three-way crossings. Once you get to the 100 Zhangwu Road campus gate on your right, follow the signs, and finally take the elevator to the 4th floor.
